What the colours mean

Simple visual guide
Inherited Influenced Emergent

Inherited

Traits that follow more direct biological inheritance patterns, such as blood type, eye colour, hair colour, skin tone range, and height tendency.

Influenced

Traits where biology can increase the likelihood, but not guarantee the outcome, such as autism, ADHD, sexuality, and gender identity.

Emergent

Traits shaped by individual variation, lived experience, and environment, such as beliefs, habits, and interests.
